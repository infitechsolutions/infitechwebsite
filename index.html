Our Offerings

PRODUCTS

Seamless Accommodation

Seamless Accommodation concentrates on making a traveler’s stay more comfortable right at the ease of his finger. The application helps the user book a room, choose a pick up, find the cab without internet, unlock or lock room doors, order in room service and also pay the bills during check out.

Connected Shopping

Connected Shopping 2.0 is a futuristic experience that a shopping centre could offer its customers. This is achieved through a branded mobile application and installation of simple gadgets at strategic locations.

Asset Tracking System

Assets are tracked through various technologies based on the need and requirement. Technology is finalized based on the size, distance of coverage, life of the battery, weight distribution and more.

Patient Tracking System

This solution reduces the wait time for patients in the waiting rooms and doctor's office by digitally granting access to information through indoor navigation capabilities.

Temperature Data Logger

Temperature Data logger, is a smart temperature monitoring system developed for checking the temperature of items in real time. This device is connected to the system through GPRS and transmits data at intervals predefined by the user.

Point of Sale device

Our POS solution acts as a cash register where cashiers input products, tally cost and conduct financial transaction. The POS software can also communicate with the inventory levels to keep everything in balance.

Wearable Solutions

We provide wearable solutions depending on requirements to record data and analyze the same.

Number Plate Recognition

This solution offers to read vehicle registration plates to create vehicle location data. It can be used to store images captured by the cameras as well as text from license plate, with some configurable to store a photograph of the driver.

Parking Solutions

This solution helps identify all spots that are free in a parking lot and those which aren’t. People would also be able to reserve their parking before reaching the mall or even make the payment through their application.

Digital Signage

Digital signage is a network of customizable displays that can be controlled electronically using a computer, allowing content to be changed remotely for the most targeted messaging possible.

SOLUTIONS

Beacon/RFID Solutions

Beacons are small Bluetooth devices that broadcast a near-constant radio signal to smart devices in the area. This signal indicates the Bluetooth user’s proximity to that beacon and, subsequently, a specific location.

AR/VR/MR Solutions

Using these technologies, the customer can access simulated objects and use them in the real world. We can transport a user from place to place without moving him/her physically.

School Bus Transport

This solution helps parents and school authorities monitor a school bus journey and also keep the kids safe from home to school and back.

Conversational Systems

Our Conversational systems solution ranges from bidirectional text or voice conversations (simple questions about the weather) to more complex interactions such as collecting oral testimony from crime witnesses to generate a sketch of a suspect.

Artificial Intelligence

We deliver AI solutions that incorporate machine learning, computer vision, natural language processing, and forecasting and optimization technologies to help you unlock new possibilities.

Chatbots

With chat bots, AI can converse with each visitor and respond to the query accordingly. This would help drive sales, convince people with services etc.

MRI

MRI can integrate a wide range of hardware devices including the tablets, touch-screens, and more. It fits seamlessly to work with existing building informational models and other 3D models thus making it the most versatile product.

2D/3D Animation

Using 2D/3D animation, one can explain process, development, presentations, concept sharing, product visualization and recreation/modeling of items that cannot be done in real life.

Projection Mapping

Projection Mapping uses everyday video projectors, but instead of projecting on a flat screen, light is mapped onto any surface. Projection mapping can be used for advertising, live concerts, theater, gaming, computing, decoration etc.
